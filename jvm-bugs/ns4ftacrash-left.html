Recipe for creating pages that crash Netscape:

  1. You need at least 5 applets (no matter what kind, here we use the most primitive applet one can write). Note that this number is platform-dependent:
    • 4 is enough in Linux and Windows 98
    • 5 is needed in IRIX 6.2 and SunOS 5.7
    • between 5 and 10 is needed in Windows NT
  2. The applets must be in tables.
  3. The WIDTH parameter of the cells containing the applets must be specified.
  4. The page must be a frame in a frameset.
You should know this recipe, to avoid it.
